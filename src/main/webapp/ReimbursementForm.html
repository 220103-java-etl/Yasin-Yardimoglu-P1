Employee Reimbursement Request Form

Instructions (Please Read)

We appreciate our customers seeking further education to advance their careers.
In an attempt to encourage our employees to further their education, we reimburse a certain
percantage of the cost associated with taking university courses, seminars, certification preparation classes,
certifications, technical training and more.

If you would like reimbursement for one of the above events, please fill out a Reimbursement Request Form.
Please wait for your financial manager to look over your request so they may approve or deny your request for reimbursement.

As a Reminder

  • All employees must complete their reimbursement form one week prior to the start of the event.
  • Each employee is allowed to claim up to $1,000 in reimbursement a year.
  • A passing grade is needed for reimbursement approval (C or above)
  • If your event's Grading Format is that of a Letter Grade format, please include the letter grade received in the Description text box below.
  • If your event's Grading Format is that of a Presentation format, please upload the video file below using the Choose File button down below.
  • If applicable and to expedite the review process, please attach any certifications, report cards, etc. files using the Choose File button down below.
  • Reimbursements do not cover course materials such as books.
